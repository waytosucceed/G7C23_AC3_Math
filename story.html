In the enchanting realm of Shapewood, three young explorers—Ava, Noah, and Zoe—embarked on a journey guided by Elder Sage. As they traversed winding paths and towering peaks, they encountered diverse structures with balanced and varying sides. When faced with a hidden valley obstacle, they collaborated, using their observations to overcome challenges and continue their quest.

By applying their knowledge of triangles Ava, Noah, and Zoe solved the valley's geometric challenge. As they stepped through the final gateway, they were greeted by Elder Sage, who commended their understanding of triangle classification and properties.
